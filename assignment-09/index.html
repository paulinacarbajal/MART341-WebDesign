Step 1: Gather Your Ingredients for the Sandwich

Collect all of your ingrediants needed for this sandwich. You're going to want:

  1. Peanut butter (crunchy or smooth, your choice)
  2. Jelly, flavor of your choice
  3. Sandwich bread (white or wheat,your choice)
  4. Plates to eat sandwich one
  5. Butter knife

Step 2: Pull Out Two Slices of Bread

Pull out two slices of bread and lay side by side on plate

Step 3: Open Peanut Butter and Jelly

Open the peanut butter and jelly and remove any added seals in order to use the product

Tip: Stir peanut butter if necessary to make smoother mixture distribute the oil that may have settled.

Step 4: Spread the Peanut Butter onto one slice of bread

Get preferred amount of peanut butter onto knife, and spread onto one slice of bread evenly
Step 5: Spread the Jelly onto the other slice of bread

Get preferred amount of jelly onto knife, and spread onto other slice of bread evenly
Step 6: Combine the Two Slices

Combine the two slices together quickly. Try to match the slices as close as possible so crust is evenly match on both sides.
Step 7:Cut off the crusts (optional)

Cut the crusts of the sandwich off using butter knife. This step is optional for those who prefer no crusts.
Step 8: Cut into half

Cut the sandwich diagnolly or in half down the middle depending on your preference.
Step 9: Clean up Workspace

Clean up youro workspace by closing the peanut butter and jelly, replacing the bread tie on the opening of the bread bag, washing your knife.
Step 10: Enjoy your Sandwich

The final step is to sit back and enjoy your sandwich! Whether you take it to go or enjoy if with a drink of your choice!
