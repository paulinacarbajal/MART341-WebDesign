About Me

 

 

Hello again, My name is Paulina Carbajal and let me give a little glimple as to who I am. I am currently a student at the University of Montana studying marketing. I am originally from Mexico, but I moved to Kalispell, MT when I was 4 years old, so I would like to say I am pretty much a Montana native at heart. I love being outdoors, exploring new places, travel enthusiast. You can always find me with spending quality time with my famiily and friends. Some areas of life that I find myself very passionate about are finding new ways to move my body, whether it be hitting the gym, going to spin class or doing some yoga & meditation. I am self-care lover and find it so important to take care of your temple. You can explore my skin-care routine, reading list, spotify playlists and get an inside look of the latest trends I am following in my virual closet.

Being a junior college can be all fun and games when you want to get the full experience, but trying to navigate who are you and who you are becoming can be very overwhelming. I love sharing personal testimonials on things that have helped me navigate adulthood and I getting some fellow wisdom from my following as well. Just in the last year I have dedicated a lot of my time into taking care of my mental health and advocating for others to talk about it learn ways to navigate this terrifying adulthood.

I love all things exploring and traveling, I have some very high hopes for possible future endeavors and a bucket list that has yet to be accomplished. I would love to share some travel tips and tricks I have learned along the way and I would love to hear any and all suggestions from others as well. I would love to share some of my favorite spots and hidden or not so hidden spots I have found along the way.
